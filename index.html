ABOUT US

Company Introduction

Kisan Laghubitta Bittiya Sanstha Ltd. (KLBSL) is a national level microfinance institution licensed by Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB), the central bank of Nepal as a ‘D’ class financial institution. This institution is incorporated as a public limited company under the Companies Act, 2063 (2006).

KLBSL has a very successful history of merger of five microfinance institutions (namely the then NRN Laghubitta, Kisan Microfinance, Dibya Laghubitta, Creative Laghubitta and Ramarosan Laghubitta) within a short span of time. The lead role of merger of these institutions were played by NRN Laghubitta Bittiya Sanstha Ltd. NRN Laghubitta was promoted by Non-Residence Nepalese, bankers, retired government officials and other reputed personalities. Initially NRN acquired two smaller size regional level microfinance institutions namely Creative and Ramarosan on 24th Bhadra 2076 (10th September 2019).
